Account Deletion Policy

At PingRents, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). If you wish to have your account and associated data permanently deleted, please follow the procedure outlined below:

  1. Send an email to admin@pingstash.com from the email address associated with your account.
  2. In the email subject, please write "Account Deletion Request" to ensure a prompt response.
  3. Once we receive your request, we will take steps to verify your identity to ensure the security of your account. This may involve asking you to provide additional information or documentation.
  4. After verifying your identity, we will proceed with the permanent deletion of your account and associated data within a reasonable timeframe, as required by GDPR.

Please note that the deletion process is irreversible, and once your account is deleted, all your personal data, including your unique identifier (email), will be permanently removed from our systems.

If you have any further questions or concerns regarding our Account Deletion Policy, please don't hesitate to contact us at admin@pingstash.com.
